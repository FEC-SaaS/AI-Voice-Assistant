### 3. Appointment Scheduling
AI agents can book appointments during calls, completely hands-free:

- **Automatic Booking**: During a call, the agent checks availability and books appointments without human intervention.
- **Calendar Integration**: Connects with Google Calendar and Outlook for real-time availability.

Every call is automatically analyzed by AI after it ends:

- **Call Transcripts**: Full transcription of every conversation — searchable and reviewable.
- **AI Summaries**: Each call gets a concise summary so managers don't have to listen to recordings.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Automatically scored as positive, neutral, or negative — spot unhappy callers before they churn.

### 6. Compliance & Regulatory
Built for businesses that need to stay on the right side of regulations:

- **TCPA Compliance**: Automated checks to ensure you're following the Telephone Consumer Protection Act.
- **Do Not Call (DNC) Management**: Maintain internal DNC lists, integrate with national DNC databases, and automatically skip numbers on the list.
- **Consent Tracking**: Record and store consent for every contact — type (prior express written consent, express consent, established business relationship), method (web form, verbal, paper), timestamp, and IP address.
- **Two-Party Consent**: Automatic detection of two-party consent states based on area code — the system knows when you need explicit recording consent.
- **Compliance Scoring**: A weighted score that tells you how compliant your operations are at a glance.
- **Audit Logs**: Every action in the system is logged — who did what, when, to which entity. Full audit trail for regulatory reviews.
- **Consent Expiration**: Track when consent expires and flag contacts that need renewed permission.
- **STIR/SHAKEN**: All managed phone numbers include STIR/SHAKEN verification to reduce spam flags on outbound calls.
- **A2P 10DLC**: Business SMS messages go through registered A2P channels so carriers don't block them.
